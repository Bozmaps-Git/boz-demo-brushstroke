What we do

Decorating, the way it should be done

From a single feature wall to a full commercial repaint, every job gets the same prep, patience and pride.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, woodwork and feature walls finished crisply, with dust sheets down and edges cut by hand.

  • Plaster & filler prep
  • Low-VOC paints
  • Same-day clean-up

Exterior & Render

Weather-tough masonry, render and timber coatings that protect your home through every Bristol winter.

  • Pressure washing
  • Repairs & pointing
  • Scaffold managed

Commercial

Offices, shops, lettings and communal areas. Out-of-hours scheduling so your business never pauses.

  • Evening & weekend
  • Minimal disruption
  • RAMS provided

Wallpaper & Murals

Feature walls, heritage papers and bespoke murals hung dead straight with seams you simply won't find.

  • Lining & sizing
  • Specialist papers

  • Kitchen respray
  • Doors & trim
  • Mirror-flat finish

Colour Consultancy

  • Home visit

  • Scheme advice
